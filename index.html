Swipe topic bar to change channel topic.

With a quick swipe to the left or your right on the topic bar, you can trigger the system to allow you to change the topic of your current channel.

Channel modes with a simple tap of a swich.

There is no need to change your modes using the keyboard. Simply go into the channel modes menu and flick a switch. If a mode requires a parameter, a dialoge will ask you for the details.

Comfortable server setup

Dump Chat takes advantage of the IRC v3.1/3.2 protocol to enhance your IRC experience. Includes support for SSL and TLS for your server and/or bouncer.

Nonintrusive notifications

Private messages, notices and error messages appear at the top of the screen without preventing you losing focus from your current conversation.

Pleasent detailed user information.

Simply tap the Detailed User Info option on an IRC user to see information about them on the active server.

Moderate your channel with ease

Someone causing a problem on your channel? Kick and ban them swiftly with a few taps on the screen.
